# Job Application Tracker

A simple Notion template designed to help job seekers organize, track, and manage their job applications in one place.

<img width="1061" height="652" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fc78567d-114c-4fb4-a526-59ade5d18131" />

### Features

- 📋 Application tracking — manage all job applications in one database
- 🏢 Company management — keep track of companies and roles applied for
- 📅 Application timeline — monitor important dates and follow-ups
- 📌 Status tracking — track application progress from applied to offer
- 📝 Notes & details — store interview notes, contacts, and important information
- 📊 Simple dashboard — get an overview of your job search progress

### Who Is This For?

Designed for:

- Students looking for internships or first jobs
- Fresh graduates managing multiple applications
- Job seekers who want a structured application system
- Professionals tracking career opportunities
